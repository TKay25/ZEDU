Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ZEDU?
Getting started is simple! Sign up with your email, choose your role (Student, Tutor, or Parent), complete your profile, and start exploring our course catalog. Our onboarding guide will walk you through everything.
What courses are available?
We offer over 500 courses across multiple subjects including Math, Science, Languages, Programming, Arts, and more. All courses are created by expert tutors and updated regularly to match current educational standards.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All new users get access to a 7-day free trial where you can explore courses and experience the platform. No credit card required. You can cancel anytime during the trial period.
How does the parent monitoring feature work?
Parents can track their child's progress, view course completion rates, and receive weekly reports. You can also communicate directly with tutors and get insights into learning patterns and areas for improvement.
How do I become a tutor on ZEDU?
Apply through our Tutor Portal. You'll need to pass our verification process and submit your credentials. Once approved, you can create courses, set pricing, and start earning immediately. We provide full support.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, digital wallets (PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay), and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted with industry-standard protocols.
